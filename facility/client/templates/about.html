About

The Faculty of Environment, coordinated by doc. Ing. Jiri Orava, Ph.D., initiated the creation of the “Research Equipment at UJEP” website.
The web helps to facilitate the visibility of research facilities located at UJEP and its partners.
It allows easy and quick search for research suites and equipment (by type or main research field),
identifying their main use, and promoting collaboration by offering the facilities to external users as well.

Authors

The website was created at the Department of Computer Science under supervision of Ing. Mgr. Pavel Beránek and Mgr. Jiří Fišer, Ph.D.
